How cap rate works

Cap rate is calculated by dividing annual net operating income by property value. It is one of the most common ways to compare income-producing real estate investments.

Formula: Cap Rate = NOI / Property Value

This calculator is for educational and preliminary analysis only. It does not account for financing, capital expenditures, reserves, leasing risk, tenant credit, or changes in market conditions.
