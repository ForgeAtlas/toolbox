How this calculator works

This calculator estimates the monthly mortgage payment using the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term. It also adds estimated monthly property taxes, insurance, PMI, and HOA fees to show a more complete monthly housing cost.

This is an estimate only. Actual mortgage payments can vary based on lender terms, taxes, insurance, escrow requirements, closing costs, and other fees.
