How DSCR works

DSCR measures whether a property's net operating income is sufficient to cover annual loan payments. A DSCR above 1.00 means NOI exceeds debt service. Many lenders look for a DSCR above 1.20 or 1.25, depending on the property type, market, borrower, and loan structure.

Formula: DSCR = NOI / Annual Debt Service

This calculator is for preliminary analysis only and does not replace lender underwriting.
